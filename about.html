Dividend and Investment Planner

By Brock Herion

I recently have gotten more into investments, specifically dividend investing. Dividend investing is the process of buying stocks that pay dividends. Then, I take those earnings and put them back into the stocks that paid them. The goal is to let the dividend compound overtime and create a form of passive income.

Currently, I am tracking all my investments in Google Sheets, which is all well and dandy, but want something a little bit more practical. So, I created this tool to help me plan out what I need to invest in and what my returns will be over a period of time.

I would eventually like to add a backend to this and possibly make it a full fledged app and add in features like

  • Individual Stock Tracking
  • Profit/Loss for a given amount of time
  • News from stocks you are following
  • Etc.

I created this tool using HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Bootstrap 4. The color theme for Bootstrap is from bootswatch.com
